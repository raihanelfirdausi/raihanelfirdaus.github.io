Project information

  • Category: Remote Sensing
  • Short Title: Map of Changes in NDVI, NDBI, and LST Values in Yogyakarta Province from 2019 to 2024
  • Affiliate: Personal Project

Description:

This map was created using Google Earth Engine with Sentinel-2 MSI satellite imagery. It illustrates the dynamics of changes in NDVI, NDBI, and LST indices from 2019 to 2024. According to the displayed legend, there has been a noticeable decline in vegetation index, alongside an increase in built-up land and surface temperature in Yogyakarta over the five-year period. These changes reflect significant environmental transformations that may impact local ecosystems and urban planning strategies.
